What practical consideration should be taken into account when listing an obese patient for surgery?
9:04
According to the Association of Anesthetists of Great Britain and Ireland, all hospitals should have named consultant anesthetists and theatre nurses responsible for the management of morbidly obese patients. These members of staff are responsible for training all staff to care for these patients and for ensuring availability of specialized equipment. All hospitals should also have local guidelines for the management of the morbidly obese patient. The patient's BMI should be added to the theatre list so that theatre staff are forewarned.
